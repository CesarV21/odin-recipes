A shrimp cocktail is a classic appetizer made with chilled, cooked shrimp served in a glass or dish with a zesty cocktail sauce. The shrimp are typically peeled and deveined, then cooked until tender. The cocktail sauce is usually a mix of ketchup, horseradish, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, and hot sauce. Garnish with lemon wedges and fresh herbs for a refreshing and flavorful start to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb cooked, peeled, and deveined shrimp
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp horseradish
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • A dash of hot sauce (optional)
  • Lemon wedges (for garnish)
  • Fresh herbs (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare the shrimp by cooking, peeling, and deveining.
  2. In a bowl, mix together ketchup, horseradish, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, and hot sauce (if using) to make the cocktail sauce.
  3. Chill the cocktail sauce and shrimp in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Arrange the chilled shrimp in individual serving glasses or a bowl.
  5. Spoon the cocktail sauce over the shrimp.
  6. Garnish with lemon wedges and fresh herbs.
  7. Serve cold and enjoy!
