Fried Fish - Cartagena Style

Ingredients

  • 1 lb white fish fillets (such as tilapia or sea bass)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Lime wedges (for serving)
  • Salsa criolla (optional, for serving)

Steps

  1. Rinse the fish fillets under cold water and pat dry with paper towels.
  2. In a shallow dish, mix together the flour, cornmeal, salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Dredge each fish fillet in the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.
  5. Carefully place the fish fillets in the hot oil and fry for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Remove the fish from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with lime wedges and salsa criolla on the side.
  8. Enjoy your delicious Cartagena-style fried fish!
